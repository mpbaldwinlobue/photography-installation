Welcome

You are entering a participatory installation about photography.

This project asks a simple question:
What deserves to be included?

You will move through three sections:

  • Process
  • Editing
  • Concept

In each section, you will see a gallery of images.

Click any image to enlarge it.
Each enlarged image contains contextual text.

You must decide:
Does this image belong in your version of the installation?

Select the works you choose to keep.

At the end, you will see the images you selected.
You may rearrange, resize, or remove them.

The final installation is yours.
